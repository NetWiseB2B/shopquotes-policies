ShopQuotes

Create, send, and manage quotes for your Shopify customers.

ShopQuotes is a Shopify app that lets merchants turn draft orders into shareable quotes, collect quote requests from customers, and send quote emails from their own connected mailbox or email service, including Gmail and Outlook.

When you connect a Google or Microsoft account, ShopQuotes uses it only to send the quote and notification emails you choose to send. We do not read your inbox and we do not use your data for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for exactly how we access, use, and protect Google user data, including our Limited Use commitments.

This site hosts our public policies. For product details, see the ShopQuotes listing on the Shopify App Store.
